# Sprint Planning

> Executed within a single hackathon day

---

## 🏗️ Sprint 1 — Foundation & Auth

### Objectives
Build the project foundation, development environment, authentication, and localization setup.

### Deliverables
- Monorepo architecture
- Docker Compose setup
- CI pipeline
- Auth system & RBAC
- Django setup
- AR/EN i18n setup

---

## ⚙️ Sprint 2 — Core Modules

### Objectives
Develop the primary business modules and administrative interfaces.

### Deliverables
- Patient CRUD & Search
- Appointment API
- User & Doctor management
- Admin/Doctor dashboards
- Patient forms

---

## 🩺 Sprint 3 — Clinical Workflow

### Objectives
Implement the patient examination and treatment workflow.

### Deliverables
- Visit API & Vitals
- Prescription API
- PDF Generation
- Examination forms
- Patient history

---

## 💰 Sprint 4 — Billing & Polish

### Objectives
Add financial management capabilities and improve reporting.

### Deliverables
- Invoice generation
- VAT & Payments
- Reports dashboard
- Charts & Analytics
- Notification system

---

## 🚀 Sprint 5 — Integration & Demo

### Objectives
Finalize the system, validate workflows, and prepare for presentation.

### Deliverables
- Deployment
- E2E & Integration testing
- Full workflow validation
- Swagger documentation
- Demo preparation

---

# Sprint Timeline Overview

| Sprint | Focus Area | Key Outcome |
|----------|------------|-------------|
| Sprint 1 | Foundation & Auth | Project setup, RBAC, localization |
| Sprint 2 | Core Modules | Patient, appointment, and user management |
| Sprint 3 | Clinical Workflow | Consultation, prescriptions, and medical records |
| Sprint 4 | Billing & Polish | Invoicing, analytics, and notifications |
| Sprint 5 | Integration & Demo | Deployment, testing, and final demo |

---

## Success Criteria

- All core clinic workflows operational
- RBAC enforced across all modules
- Arabic & English localization available
- Appointment lifecycle fully functional
- Prescription PDFs generated successfully
- Billing and reporting modules completed
- End-to-end workflow tested and validated
- Production deployment completed
